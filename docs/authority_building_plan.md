# Authority Building Plan

## Objectives

- Increase quality referring domains from regional/local trade-relevant sources.
- Improve topical authority around electrical safety, panel upgrades, EV charging, and standby power.

## Workstreams

1. **Local citations & NAP consistency**
   - Audit and normalize business name, phone, service area across major local directories.
2. **Link gap acquisition**
   - Target chambers, builder associations, property management resources, and local publications.
3. **Unlinked mention reclamation**
   - Monitor brand mentions and request link attribution.
4. **Authority assets**
   - Publish practical calculators/checklists and seasonal preparedness guides.

## KPI targets (90 days)

- +8 net new quality referring domains
- +15% non-branded clicks to service pages
- +10% branded SERP CTR uplift
