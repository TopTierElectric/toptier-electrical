_Last updated_: April 19, 2026

Top Tier Electrical respects your privacy. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how to contact us with questions.

## Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to visitors and customers using:

- [https://toptier-electrical.com](https://toptier-electrical.com)
- Contact, booking, and quote request forms hosted on this website

## Information we collect

We may collect:

- Contact details you submit (name, phone, email, address, and project details)
- Appointment preferences and service request notes
- Basic technical and analytics data (pages viewed, browser/device type, referring source)

We do **not** intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless you include it in a message.

## How we use information

We use your information to:

- Respond to your inquiry or quote request
- Schedule and provide electrical services
- Send service-related communications (confirmation, follow-up, scheduling changes)
- Improve website performance, content quality, and user experience
- Maintain security and prevent spam or abuse

## Form processing and service providers

We use third-party tools to operate this website and process forms/analytics. Those providers process data on our behalf only as needed to deliver their services.

Examples include hosting, form handling, and analytics providers.

## Cookies and analytics

This website may use cookies or similar technologies for:

- Essential site functionality
- Measuring page performance and traffic patterns
- Improving content relevance and call-to-action clarity

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## Data retention

We retain inquiry and customer records only as long as reasonably necessary for:

- Customer support and service delivery
- Legal, accounting, and operational requirements

When no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymized where practical.

## Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information only when necessary to:

- Deliver requested services
- Work with trusted service providers
- Comply with legal obligations or enforce our rights

## Your choices

You may request to:

- Access or correct your information
- Ask us to delete information we are not required to keep
- Opt out of non-essential communications

## Children’s privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes are posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
